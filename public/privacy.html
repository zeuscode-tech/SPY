Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 27, 2026

Spy Ultimate is an offline party game. The app is designed to work without accounts, advertising, analytics SDKs, or a remote gameplay server.

Data Stored On Device

The app may save player names, custom words, selected settings, and recent word or spy history on your device. This data is used only to run the game and improve local word rotation.

Data Collection

Spy Ultimate does not collect, sell, or share personal data with third parties. Gameplay data is not sent to a remote server.

Permissions

The Android app declares internet access for the embedded web runtime and offline web app shell. The app does not use this permission for ads, analytics, tracking, or account features.

Children

Spy Ultimate is a party game intended for general audiences. It does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Deletion

You can delete locally stored game data by clearing the app data in Android system settings or uninstalling the app.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer using the contact email shown on the app's Google Play listing.
